Title: Lentil Feta Crepes
Description: Recipe from the back of a can of lentils
Source: Westbrae Lentils
Original URL: http://www.westbrae.com/recipes/index.php
Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/ddcd603ecdad4b0689ee777445f26906.jpg
Ingredients:
	3 tbsp olive oil
	3 shallots, finely chopped
	8 oz mushrooms, thinly sliced
	1 can lentils
	6 crepes
	4 oz low fat feta cheese, crumbled
	10 to 12 kalamata olives, chopped

Instructions:
	wrap crepes in foil & warm in oven
	heat oil in skillet
	sautee shallots and mushrooms until soft and golden
	add lentils and heat through
	add splash of cream, broth, or win to moisten lentils if desired
	fill crepes with lentil mixture & feta cheese
	roll up crepe
	serve with olives sprinkled on top
