Title: Spicy Black Bean Soup
Description:
Source: Cookie + Kate
Original URL: https://cookieandkate.com/2016/spicy-vegan-black-bean-soup/
Yield: 4
Active: 15min
Total: 60min
Categories: soup, vegan, blender
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/9380a9a30a0e47cfa4bb65f61b9c3834.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 medium yellow onions, chopped
	3 celery ribs, chopped fine
	1 large carrot, peeled and chopped
	1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
	3/4 teaspoons ground cumin
	1/2 teaspoon ground jalapeno or cayenne
	3 (15-ounce) cans of black beans, rinsed and drained
	1 cups water or more
	1 teaspoon Better Than Boullion
	1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro (optional)
	1 teaspoon vinegar or 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice

[Optional garnishes]
	diced avocado, extra cilantro, thinly sliced radishes, tortilla chipsâ€¦

Instructions:
	Cook down the onions, celery and carrot until soft, about 10 to 15 minutes.
	Add in spices and stir for 30 seconds
	Pour in the beans and broth and bring to a gentle simmer.
	Simmer until the broth is flavorful and the beans are very tender, about 30 minutes.
	Take it off the heat and use the immersion blender + water to your liking to get it your preferred consistency.
