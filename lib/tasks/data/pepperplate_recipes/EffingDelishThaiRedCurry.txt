Title: Effing Delish Thai Red Curry
Description: This recipe is simple and based on what i saw in a yellow curry the last time we amazing curry at Thailicisous in Slidell, LA
Source: Anne's Imagination
Original URL: 
Yield: 4
Active: 20
Total: 30
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/512184c03e4a44a89b11ef5ae6bc2731.jpg
Ingredients:
	1/2 jar Thai red curry paste
	1 can thick coconut milk
	3 sticks celery, sliced at angle
	2 medium carrots, sliced thinly
	1 box of frozen jackfruit (thai curry, if you'd like), thawed
	3 radishes, chopped (or something similar in texture)
	1 onion, cut in mediumish chunks
	1 block cubed tofu (optional)
	1 potato, cubed, pre-steamed (optional)

Instructions:
	make rice
	chop & sautee veg
	add in jackfruit
	pour in coconut milk
	stir in curry paste
	put on low and simmer for about 10 min
	serve over rice
