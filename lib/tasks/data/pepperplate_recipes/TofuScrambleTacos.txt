Title: Tofu Scramble Tacos
Description: 3 meals for two people. Serve with tortillas & avocado. Cheese is a nice addition.
Source: The Kitchn
Original URL: http://www.thekitchn.com/recipe-southwestern-tofu-scramble-recipes-from-the-kitchn-183466
Yield: 6 servings
Active: 45 min
Total: 45 min
Categories: tofu
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/4022fa04f67041f195a5740381ec93ec.jpg
Ingredients:
[Cans]
	1 16-ounce package firm tofu
	1 can black beans
	2 tortillas each

[Fresh Veg]
	1/2 cup diced white onion (from 1/2 small onion)
	3/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper (from 1 small pepper)
	1/2 cup finely chopped Anaheim pepper (from 1 medium pepper)
	1 ripe tomato, diced
	3 green onions, chopped
	1/4 bunch fresh cilantro
	1 sliced avocado

[Spices]
	1/4 teaspoon coriander
	1/2 teaspoon cumin
	1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
	1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste at the end
	1 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric

Instructions:
	Press or squeeze water out of tofu
	Crumble the tofu and set aside in a bowl
	Prep your spices
	Prep all of your Veg
	Drain and rinse the beans
	In the large skillet, sautee the onion, red bell pepper, and anaheim pepper
	When the onion is translucent, add beans and tofu
	Add in your spices, tomatoes, and stir thoroughly.
	Put the lid on and let the tomatoes cook down a little (simmer medium low). If it is looking a little dry, add 2 tablespoons of water and put the lid back on.
	Start heating the tortillas.
	When the tomatoes are softened, turn off the heat, add in the green onions and cilantro.
	When the tortillas are done, serve with avocado and salsa
