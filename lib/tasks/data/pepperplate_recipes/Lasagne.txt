Title: Lasagne
Description: easy to prep in advance
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 6
Active: 
Total: 3 hours
Categories: pasta, long cook time
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/d07c056b912a47339369cff80397df1a.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 block tofu
	4 Anaheim peppers
	1 tablespoon basil
	1 tablespoon oregano
	1 teaspoon marjoram
	1/2 onion, chopped
	1 clove garlic, minced
	1 cup parmasean cheese
	2 jar pasta sauce
	6 strips of Vigo precooked lasagne noodles (1/2 8 oz pkg)

Instructions:
	Use either one 8x8 square dish or two 4x9 dishes
	Sautee onion, garlic, anaheim
	While veg is cooking, squeeze & crumble tofu into a bowl
	Mix in parmesan and spices: basil, oregano, marjoram
	When veg is done, mix into the bowl
	Spray oil on a pie plate or glass baking dish
	Put a little sauce on bottom
	then layer in noodle --> mixture --> sauce, 3 times
	Bake for 60 minutes
	Serve with crusty bread & salad
