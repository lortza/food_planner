Title: Thai Red Curry with Vegetables
Description: 
Source: Cookie + Kate
Original URL: http://cookieandkate.com/2015/thai-red-curry-recipe/
Yield: Serves 4 servings
Active: 10 mins
Total: 40 mins
Categories: kale, thai, new
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/17b78c463d384e629514928cd896ced9.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 cup uncooked rice
	1 small white onion, diced
	1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
	2 cloves garlic, minced
	1 red bell pepper, sliced into thin 2-inch long strips
	1 yellow or green bell pepper, sliced into thin 2-inch long strips
	3 carrots, peeled and sliced on the diagonal into 1/4-inch wide rounds (to yield about 1 cup sliced carrots)
	2 oz Thai red curry paste (half of the little jar)
	1 can (14 ounces) full-fat coconut milk
	1 1/2 cups packed thinly sliced kale (tough ribs removed first), preferably the Tuscan/lacinato/dinosaur variety
	1 1/2 teaspoons coconut sugar or turbinado (raw) sugar or brown sugar
	2 teaspoons soy sauce
	1 1/2 teaspoons rice or white vinegar
	1 handful of cilantro, chopped
	8 large basil leaves, chopped
	1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
	Optional sriracha or chili garlic sauce

Instructions:
	Cook the rice (put 1 cup rice and 1.5 cups water into medium sauce pan and bring to a boil. Turn down to simmer for about 20 min)
	In the soup pot (frying pan is too small), sautee the onion, garlic, and ginger, stirring continuously
	Add the bell peppers and carrots and cook for until they are fork-tender, 3 to 5 more minutes
	Then add the curry paste and crushed red pepper and cook, stirring often, for 2 minutes.
	Add the coconut milk and kale to the pan along with 1/4 cup water (if needed, depending on desired liquid) and 1.5 teaspoons sugar.
	Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until the peppers, carrots and kale have softened to your liking, about 5 to 10 minutes.
	Turn off heat and add rice vinegar and soy sauce
	Add salt (1/4 teaspoon), basil, and cilantro
	Serve on top of rice.
