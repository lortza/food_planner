Ingredients:
[Select one of the following combinations and proceed with recipe:]
	2 cups sliced bell peppers + 2 sliced shallots
	2 cups corn kernels + 1/2 cup chopped green onions
	2 cups zucchini ribbons + 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers + 1 green onion
	2 cups blueberries + 1/2 cup sliced red onions

[The brine]
	2/3 cup rice vinegar
	1/3 cup water
	1/3 cup sugar
	2 teaspoon kosher salt
	2 cloves garlic, crushed
	2 pods star anise

Instructions:
	Place vegetable or berries in a non-reactive heat-proof bowl
	In a small sauce pan, combine vinegar, water, sugar, salt, garlic, and anise.
	Bring to a boil, stirring until salt/sugar is dissolved
	Pour the hot pickle brine over the vegetables or berries
	Cover with plastic and set aside to cool down to room temp
	Transfer bowl contents to a jar, cover tightly and refrigerate up to 2 weeks.
