Title: Spicy Sweet Potato Mac N’ Cheese
Description: 
Source: Farmers Market Foods
Original URL: http://www.farmersmarketfoods.com/recipes/spicy-sweet-potato-mac-n-cheese/
Yield: 6
Active: 
Total: 1 hour
Categories: chorizo
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/f6d6729eb44f4a34a370597e05da7658.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 can Sweet Potato Puree
	8 ounces uncooked elbow shape macaroni pasta
	1 cup milk
	2 tablespoons chopped jalapeño
	0.5 to 1 tsp crushed red pepper
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
	1 tablespoon olive oil
	1 cup (or more) shredded Cheddar Cheese
	1/2 lb Mexican style chorizo, cut into small dice
	1/4 cup chopped green onions
	1/4 bunch of cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
[If you're using an Oven...]
	Lube up the big (9x13) glass baking dish
	Preheat broiler.

[If you're using a Toaster Oven...]
	Lube up 2 of the loaf-shaped rectangle aluminum baking trays

[For either oven...]
	Cook pasta according to package directions.
	Get out the large metal bowl and a silicon spatula.
	In the metal bowl, combine sweet potato puree, chorizo, milk, salt, jalapeño, pepper, and cheese.
	Add cooked & drained macaroni to the mixture.
	Put mixture in chosen baking dish
	Broil until lightly browned and crisp.
	Sprinkle with onions and cilantro.
