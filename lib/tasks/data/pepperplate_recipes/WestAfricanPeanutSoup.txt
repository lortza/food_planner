Ingredients:
	1 tsp vegetable oil
	1 onion, peeled / diced
	2 garlic cloves, peeled / minced
	2 inch piece fresh ginger, peeled / chopped
	5 cups vegetable stock
	1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes in juice
	1/2 cup peanut butter
	4 cups peeled / diced sweet potatoes (2 med potatoes)
	1 tbsp lightly packed dark brown sugar
	1/2 tsp cayenne pepper, more if desired

Instructions:
	In the large soup pot, cook the onion, garlic, ginger.
	Add the sweet potato and cook until is is soft. (put the lid on and use a little bit of broth for steam)
	Add in the tomatoes, broth, peanut butter, cayenne, brown sugar and bring to a boil.
	Blend in batches. Vent the steam while blending.
	Makes 2 large jars + 2 servings.
