Title: SNAP Challenge: Vegetable Stir Fry with Noodles
Description:
Source: BudgetBytes
Original URL: http://www.budgetbytes.com/2014/09/snap-challenge-vegetable-stir-fry-noodles/
Yield: Serves 6 (about 1.25 cups each)
Active: 15 mins
Total: 25 mins
Categories: stirfry, noodle, cabbage
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/382a71e7811c4046ab3cbae33fc33444.jpg
Ingredients:
	1/2 small head purple cabbage $0.91
	2 green bell peppers $1.00
	2 carrots, peeled into strips
	2 celery stick, cut into angle slabs
	1 yellow onion
	1 package of glass noodles (equal to 2 ramen noodle pkgs)
	1/4 cup liquid aminos
	3 teaspoon brown sugar
	3 tbsp vinegar
	1/4 teaspoon cayenne
	3 green onions
	handful cilantro
	handful peanuts
	handful sunflower seeds
	sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:
	In a small bowl prepare the sauce by stirring together the liquid aminos, brown sugar, cayenne, and vinegar
	Cook and Drain the cooked noodles in a colander.
	Cut all veg into strips
	Saut√© the veg until in the soup pot they just begin to soften.
	When the veg and noodles are done, combine everything in the soup pot. add in green onion, nuts, cilantro. mix carefully with tongs. serve. top with sesame seeds.
