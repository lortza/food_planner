Title: Gluten Free Cornbread Mix
Description: These are the instructions from the bag of cornbread mix. The bag is gone b/c mouse prevention. These instructions are for a whole bag. Which is 1 lb of mix or 2 pint jars. We currently have 1 pint jar, which is a 1/2 recipe. Use the recipe scale to do the math.
Source: Bob's Red Mill
Original URL: http://www.bobsredmill.com/recipes/how-to-make/gluten-free-cornbread-mix-instructions/
Yield: 12
Active: 30 min
Total: 40 min
Categories: bake, cornbread, oven
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/5908b1f96c8a4f8c83cb46aa704f01cd.jpg
Ingredients:
	20 oz Gluten Free Cornbread Mix (3-1/2 cups)
	1.5 cups Milk
	2 Eggs
	1/2 cup melted Butter or Oil

Instructions:
	Have all the ingredients at room temperature.
	Preheat oven to 375°F.
	Generously grease a 9 x 9-inch pan.
	Place cornbread mix in large bowl.
	Add milk, eggs and butter or oil.
	Mix until blended, then mix vigorously for 30 seconds more.
	Pour into prepared baking pan.
	Bake until golden-brown, about 25 minutes.

[For cornbread muffins]
	Line muffin pan with paper baking cups, or spray with non-stick cooking spray.
	Fill cups 3/4-full with batter.
	Bake at 375°F until tops are golden-brown, about 20 minutes.
	Makes 12 muffins.
