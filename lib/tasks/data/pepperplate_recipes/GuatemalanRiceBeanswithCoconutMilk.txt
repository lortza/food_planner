Title: Guatemalan Rice & Beans with Coconut Milk
Description:
Source: Goya
Original URL: https://www.goya.com/en/recipes/guatemalan-rice-and-beans-with-coconut-milk
Yield: 4
Active: 30
Total: 30
Categories: beans, rice, guatemalan
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/75dcbb044f1042f6958809c1589f26af.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 tbsp. Corn Oil
	1/4 cup onion, diced
	1 teaspoon. Minced Garlic or 2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
	1 1/2 cups cooked CANILLA® Long Grain Rice
	1 1/2 cups water
	1 can (13.5 oz.) Coconut Milk
	1/8 teaspoon. Thyme leaf
	1 packet Sazón without Annatto (garlic, cumin, corriander)
	1 teaspoon. Adobo with Pepper
	1 can (15.5 oz.) Small Red Beans, drained and rinsed
	1 red bell pepper

Instructions:
	In saucepan, heat oil on medium. Stir in onion, pepper and garlic and cook, stirring often, for 5 minutes.
	Add rice and cook for 2 minutes stirring constantly. Stir in water. Stir in coconut milk, thyme, Sazon, and Adobo and bring to a broil. Stir in beans and return to a boil. Cover and lower heat to low. Simmer for 25 minutes. Stir every 5 or 10 minutes to avoid sticking.
	When rice is tender, remove from heat, fluff with a fork, cover and let rice sit for 5 minutes. Serve with desired main course.
