Title: Guacamole
Description: Best served with yellow corn tortilla chips
Source: Anne's Imagination
Original URL: 
Yield: 1
Active: 10 min
Total: 10 min
Categories: snack, avocado
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/e4c8165cf2c9455381635c588b70e14d.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 avocado
	1/2 small onion, finely diced
	1/2 tsp garlic salt
	1 tbs fresh chilantro, finely chopped
	1 wedge lime juice

Instructions:
	Mash the avocado in a bowl with a fork.
	Mix in the onion, salt, cilantro, and lime juice.
	Serve fresh.
