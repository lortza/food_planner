Title: Red Lentil Curry
Description: This recipe has been modified from the original to bring the spice amounts into balance
Source: Jessica in the Kitchen
Original URL: https://jessicainthekitchen.com/red-lentil-curry-vegan/
Yield: 4
Active: 30
Total: 60
Categories: curry
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/5b8972b2e1344a83965f7a248ec4f978.jpg
Ingredients:
	2 medium yellow onions OR 1 red onion (7g)
	2 cups fresh tomatoes, diced (if using canned, do not drain)
	1/4 tsp sea salt
	1/4 tsp ground black pepper
	1/4 tsp garlic powder
	1 tablespoon freshly minced ginger
	1/2 tsp garam masala
	3/4 tsp curry powder
	1/4 tsp cumin
	1 tablespoon red curry paste
	1 cup dry red lentils, rinsed
	2 cups filtered water
	1 can coconut milk
	1/2 cup dry rice (cook separately)

[garnish]
	1 small lime
	red pepper flakes
	chopped parsley

Instructions:
	Cook down the onions and tomatoes
	Add in the spices, simmer for about 30 seconds
	Add in the lentils & water and bring to a boil.
	Be careful to stir enough to keep the lentils from sticking to the bottom.
	Bring the temp down and a simmer for 30 min.
	When the lentils are soft, turn off the heat and add in the coconut milk.

Notes: This is spicy and sweet. It's really nice.
