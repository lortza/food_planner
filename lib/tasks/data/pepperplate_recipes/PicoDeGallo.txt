Title: Pico De Gallo
Description:
Source: Anne's Imagination
Original URL:
Yield: 1
Active: 10 min
Total: 10 min
Categories: snack, mexican
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/8a37d3f64ce340d6809fe1260706289b.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 large tomato
	1/2 small onion
	1 clove garlic
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1 jalapeño
	2 sprigs fresh cilantro

Instructions:
	Try a small piece of the jalapeño. Depending on its heat, include as much as you'd like, finely diced.
	Finely chop the onion and tomato.
	Finely mince the garlic.
	Chop the cilantro leaves into tiny pieces.
	Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
	Let it sit for 20 minutes.
	Enjoy with tortilla chips.
