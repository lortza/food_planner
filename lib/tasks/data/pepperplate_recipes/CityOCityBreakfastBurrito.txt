Title: City O City Breakfast Burrito
Description: We used to love the breakfast burritos we could get to go. This recreates them.
Source: 
Original URL: 
Yield: 6
Active: 30
Total: 30
Categories: tofu, burrito
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/49fb0f90f37f4c5190840f7565b6d821.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 16-ounce package firm tofu, crumbled
	1 large tortillas each
	1 large potato, cubed and steamed
	1/2 cup diced white onion (from 1/2 small onion)
	3/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper (from 1 small pepper)
	1/2 cup finely chopped Anaheim pepper (from 1 medium pepper)

[Spices]
	1/4 teaspoon coriander
	1/2 teaspoon cumin
	1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
	1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste at the end
	1 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric

[Fixins]
	salsa
	shredded cheese OR yogurt
	green onions

Instructions:
	Cube and bake the potato
	Chop and sautee: onion, bell pepper, anaheim
	Once softened, mix in crumbled tofu and add spices
	When potatoes are done, mix them into the tofu
	Use the Toaster oven to soften the large tortillas
	Put each one on a plate, fill with mixture and fixins.
	Roll & top with salsa.
