Title: Healing Cabbage Soup

Yield: lots
Active: 45 min
Total: 1 hour
Categories: soup, cabbage
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/390578acd07a4bba8198ad2ad7c5764d.jpg
Ingredients:
	3 tablespoons olive oil
	1/2 onion, chopped
	2 cloves garlic, chopped
	8 cups (2 quarts) broth
	1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
	1/2 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
	1/2 head cabbage, cored and coarsely chopped (freeze other half in gallon ziplock)
	1 (14.5 ounce) can Italian-style stewed tomatoes, drained and diced

Instructions:
	In a large stockpot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Stir in onion and garlic; cook until onion is transparent, about 5 minutes.
	Stir in water, bouillon, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then stir in cabbage. Simmer until cabbage wilts, about 10 minutes.
	Stir in tomatoes. Return to a boil, then simmer 15 to 30 minutes, stirring often.
