Title: Lentil Sloppy Joe Sliders
Description:
Source: Chocolate Covered Katie
Original URL: http://chocolatecoveredkatie.com/2012/10/26/super-healthy-lentil-sloppy-joes/
Yield: 12 sliders
Active: 30 min
Total: 30 min
Categories: lentils, bbq
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/ea178430917249cba6945c5b25ca8763.jpg
Ingredients:
	12 slider buns
	1 tub potato salad
	1/2 an onion (diced) and 2 teaspoon oil
	1 clove minced garlic
	1 15 oz can pigeon peas, drained & rinsed
	1 15-oz cans lentils, drained and rinsed
	1 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
	1 tablespoon ground cumin
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
	3 to 5 teaspoon sugar or to taste
	14 oz tomato sauce (I used no-salt-added)
	1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
	2 tablespoon soy sauce (or soy-free alternative)
	up to 1 cup water, depending on how much you need

Instructions:
	saut√© the onion and garlic in the oil until onion is translucent. Add lentils and spices, stir, then add all other ingredients and cook uncovered (stirring occasionally) until thick like sloppy joe filling.
