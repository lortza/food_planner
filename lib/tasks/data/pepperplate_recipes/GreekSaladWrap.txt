Title: Greek Salad Wrap
Description: Make 30-60 min ahead of time so it can marinate. (This recipe has been modified to our suiting)
Source: Simply Recipes
Original URL: http://www.simplyrecipes.com/recipes/dads_greek_salad/
Yield: Serves 6
Active: 15 minutes
Total: 60 minutes
Categories: salad, summer
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/488144dc96a7445b97d1498d855e51d5.jpg
Ingredients:
[Vegetables]
	3 large plum tomatoes, seeded, diced
	3/4 cucumber, peeled, seeded, diced
	1/2 red onion, peeled, minced
	1 bell pepper, seeded, coarsely chopped
	1/2 cup pitted black olives (preferably brine-cured), coarsely chopped
	1 clove garlic, minced

[Liquids]
	6 Tbsp olive oil
	1 fresh lemon, juiced
	1 teaspoon red wine vinegar

[Spices]
	3/4 teaspoon dried oregano
	1 teaspoon dried dill
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	8 cranks of freshly ground black pepper

[Accessories]
	2 large (8-inch) tortillas, warmed
	1/8 cup crumbled feta cheese per tortilla
	2 Tbsp yogurt per tortilla
	1/4 cup salad greens per wrap

Instructions:
	Combine the tomatoes, cucumber, onion, bell pepper, olives, in a bowl.
	Add olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, vinegar, oregano, dill weed, salt, and pepper.
	Stir and let sit at room temp for 30-60 min.
	Warm a tortilla, then fill with the mixture.
	Top with feta, yogurt, and some salad greens.
	Wrap it up and eat it. Yum.
