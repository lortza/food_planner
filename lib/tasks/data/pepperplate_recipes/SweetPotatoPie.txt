Title: Sweet Potato Pie
Description: This is the recipe from the can.
Source: Farmers Market Foods
Original URL: http://www.farmersmarketfoods.com/recipes/sweet-potato-pie/
Yield: 1 pie
Active: 15 min
Total: 75 min
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/c755023d54ca4f9f8e2bb95ccb5b5e66.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 15 oz. can Farmer’s Market Sweet Potato Puree
	3/4 cup sugar
	1/2 teaspoon. ground nutmeg
	1/4 teaspoon. ground ginger
	1 teaspoon. vanilla
	1 9”, deep dish pie shell

Instructions:
	Mix sweet potato with remaining ingredients.
	Pour into pie shell.
	Bake at 425º for 15 minutes
	Reduce temperature to 350º for an additional 35-40 minutes.
	Thoroughly cool pie before serving. Serves 6-8.
