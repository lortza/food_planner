Title: Busy-Day Chocolate Cake
Description: Makes 1 pie plate of cake. Perfect fit!
Source: MarthaStewart
Original URL: http://www.marthastewart.com/334278/busy-day-chocolate-cake
Yield:
Active: 15 min
Total: 1 hr
Categories: cake, chocolate
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/a561070f59c3416fb61c49dd523b8a99.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, (spooned and leveled)
	1 cup sugar
	6 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
	1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
	1/4 teaspoon chili powder
	1/8 teaspoon cayenne
	1 teaspoon baking soda
	1/4 teaspoon salt
	1/3 cup liquid coconut oil
	1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
	1 tablespoon white vinegar
	1 cup cold water

Instructions:
	Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
	Whisk together 1.5 cups flour, 1 cup sugar, 3 tablespoon cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon baking soda, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
	Make a well in center of flour mixture and add 6 tablespoon vegetable oil,  1 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1 tablespoon white vinegar, and 1 cup cold water.
	Whisk until well combined.
	Pour into  an 8-inch square baking pan.
	Bake until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.
	Let cool completely in pan on a wire rack.
