Title: Cajun & Creole Seasoning
Description: The main difference between Cajun and Creole seasoning is that Creole city folks add oregano to the blend, a legacy of Italian immigrants to New Orleans. Add 1 tablespoon of dried oregano to the recipe above.
Source: Allrecipes.com
Original URL: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/149221/cajun-spice-mix/
Yield: 12 servings
Active: 5 minutes
Total: 5 minutes
Categories: spices, cajun, new orleans, creole
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/1354689e4b6d4f0eb5e5305a8e96f682.jpg
Ingredients:
	2 teaspoons salt
	1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
	1 teaspoon ground black pepper
	2 1/2 teaspoons paprika
	2 teaspoons garlic powder
	1 teaspoon onion powder
	1 1/4 teaspoons dried thyme
	1 1/4 teaspoons dried oregano
	1 teaspoon dried basil
	1 teaspoon celery seed
	1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:
	Stir together salt, garlic powder, paprika, black pepper, onion powder, cayenne pepper, oregano, thyme, and red pepper flakes until evenly blended. Store in an airtight container.
