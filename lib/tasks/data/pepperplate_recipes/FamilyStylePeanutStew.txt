Title: Family Style Peanut Stew
Description: 
Source: Once Again Nut Butters
Original URL: https://www.onceagainnutbutter.com/peanut-butter
Yield: 4
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: peanut butter
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/67027beb9aa1440aac24ebc91189fb0a.jpg
Ingredients:
	1/2 cup uncooked rice (1 cup water)
	4 tomatoes, cut into 8 wedges or 1 28-ounce (795g) can diced tomatoes
	3 to 4 cups (720 to 960 ml) chopped green cabbage
	2 sweet potatoes or yams, cut into 3/4-inch chunks
	2 carrots, sliced
	1 onion cut vertically into half-moon slices
	2 cloves garlic, minced
	4 cups vegetable broth, divided6
	6 oz tomato paste
	1/3 cup raisins
	1 teaspoon salt
	1/4 teaspoon pepper
	3/4 cup natural unsalted peanut butter, smooth or crunchy

Instructions:
	Make the rice: put rice and water in the larger sauce pan, bring to boil, then simmer on low for about 15 min.
	In a large deep skillet or stockpot, combine the tomatoes, cabbage, sweet potatoes, carrots, onion, and garlic.
	Pour in 3 cups of the vegetable broth and bring to a boil over high heat.
	Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently, then reduce the heat to medium and simmer 10 to 15 minutes, or until the carrots and sweet potatoes are fork tender.
	Add the remaining 1 cup of vegetable broth along with the tomato paste, raisins, salt and pepper and stir well.
	Cook for 1 minute, then add the peanut butter and stir until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
	Put some rice in a bowl and spoon the stew over it.
