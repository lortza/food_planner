Title: Simple Sesame Noodles
Description: NEED TO ADD TOFU OR MUSHROOMS OR SOMETHING OF SUBSTANCE TO THIS RECIPE
Source: Food Network
Original URL: http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/ree-drummond/simple-sesame-noodles.html
Yield: 
Active: 10 minutes
Total: 20 minutes
Categories: new, pasta
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/07916ccc872746c0a79f879eee1efbfb.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 package tofu cubes, baked in advance (optional)
	1 can mushrooms, drained, sauteed <-- crazy new idea. let's try this.
	12 ounces angel hair pasta
	1/4 cup soy sauce
	1/4 cup canola oil
	3 tablespoons pure sesame oil
	2 tablespoons sugar
	2 tablespoons rice vinegar
	1/2 teaspoon hot chili oil
	4 cloves garlic, minced
	4 green onions, thinly sliced

Instructions:
	Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
	Meanwhile, whisk together the soy sauce, canola oil, sesame oil, sugar, vinegar, chili oil and garlic in a bowl.
	Heat the tofu
	Brown the mushrooms in a pan.
	Taste and adjust the ingredients as needed.
	Drain the noodles.
	Combine everything back in the now empty noodle cooking pot.
	Add the green onions and toss.
	Serve in a bowl with chopsticks.
