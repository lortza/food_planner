Title: Curried-Cashew Cauliflower
Description: This recipe has been modified to resolve the too-much-curry cluck
Source: Once Again Nut Butters
Original URL: https://www.onceagainnutbutter.com/cashew-butter
Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: curry, peanut, cauliflower
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/cd45427a68a64ab7875fe417042039df.jpg
Ingredients:
	1/2 cup uncooked rice
	1 head cauliflower, cut into large florets (or big bag of thawed frozen)
	1/2 cup Once Again Organic Cashew Butter
	2 tbsp olive oil
	3/4 tbsp curry powder
	1/4 tsp kosher salt
	1/2 tsp crushed pepper
	1/2 cup salted cashews or mixed nuts, roughly chopped

Instructions:
	Cook the rice (1/2 cup rice, 1 cup water. throw it all in together, bring to a boil, then simmer for about 15 min)
	Preheat the oven to 350F.
	Mix together the cashew butter, curry powder, olive oil and salt.
	Toss the cauliflower and chopped cashews in the curry mixture.
	Spread out the cauliflower in a single layer on a cookie sheet.
	Bake the cauliflower for about 25 min, you may need to stir, every 10 min to get the peanut butter evenly coated
