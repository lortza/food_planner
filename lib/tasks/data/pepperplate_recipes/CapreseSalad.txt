Title: Caprese Salad
Description: pick up a crusty bread to have with this
Source: Anne's Imagination
Original URL: 
Yield: 4 servings
Active: 10 min
Total: 15 min
Categories: basil, salad
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/1b6703e8eae84086b4b7efba983514b5.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 package of mozzarella pearls
	3 ripe creole tomatoes, diced
	10 fresh basil leaves, chopped
	1/2 tsp salt
	1 TBSP balsamic vinegar
	1 serving (each) of salad greens
	1 baguette boulangerie bread

Instructions:
	combine everything together in a bowl and let it sit for several minutes.
	serve as-is or over mixed greens.
