Title: Mexican Pizza
Description: We had a pizza like this when we lived in Todos Santos. Damn it's good!

Ingredients:
	1 large boboli crust
	1 can amy's refried beans
	1 bag shredded mexican cheese
	1 tomato
	1 jalapeno

Instructions:
	Follow instructions on Boboli
	OR
	preheat oven to 425F
	coat boboli with nearly all of the beans
	cover with cheese
	top with tomato slices and jalapeno slices
	bake for 10 min or so
