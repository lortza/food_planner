Title: Lebanese Lentils, Rice and Caramelized Onions (Mujadara)
Description:
Source: Food Network
Original URL: http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/aarti-sequeira/lebanese-lentils-rice-and-caramelized-onions-mujadara-recipe.html
Yield: 4
Active: 15 minutes
Total: 45 min
Categories: lebanese, lentils, rice
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/9c8bbc99ba9c446fb637f7f290f3700b.jpg
Ingredients:
	1 can lentils, drained rinsed
	1 teaspoon cumin seeds
	1/2 teaspoon cracked black peppercorns
	2 medium red onions, thinly sliced
	1/2 teaspoon sea salt
	3/4 cup basmati rice
	2 cups broth
	1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
	1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
	1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
	1/2 teaspoon turmeric
	1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
	2 tablespoons pine nuts, optional

Instructions:
	In the big soup pot
	Cook the onions until translucent
	add spices
	add dry rice
	mix well
	add lentils & broth
	bring to a boil then simmer on low for about 20 min
