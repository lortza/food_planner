Ingredients:
	1/3 cup toasted coconut flakes
	1/2 yellow onion, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
	1/2 green bell pepper, chopped (about 1/2 cup)
	2 cloves garlic, minced
	1 can (15 oz.) Green Pigeon Peas
	1 packet Saz√≥n with Coriander and Annatto: salt, garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika
	4 oz. Tomato Sauce
	3 cups water
	2 cups rice

Instructions:
	Toast coconut flakes in a dry skillet until they start to get a little brown
	In large skillet, cook onions and peppers, cook for 3 minutes
	Stir in garlic
	Add gandules, tomato sauce, water, and bring to a boil
	Add coconut flakes and rice
	Stir, cover, reduce to simmer for 25 min
